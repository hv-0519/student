About ExamPro

Learn more about our innovative online examination system built with cutting-edge technology.

ExamPro is a comprehensive online examination platform designed to provide secure, fair, and efficient assessments for educational institutions and organizations. Our system leverages modern web technologies and machine learning to ensure integrity in online testing.

Created in 2026, ExamPro was developed as a real-world project to demonstrate full-stack development skills, including backend with Flask, machine learning for cheating detection, and interactive frontend interfaces.

Our mission is to make online examinations accessible, reliable, and insightful while maintaining privacy and focusing on educational improvement rather than punishment.

Key highlights:
- Built by aspiring developers for internship portfolios
- Focus on explainable AI and user-friendly design
- Open for contributions and improvements
